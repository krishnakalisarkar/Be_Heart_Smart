What affects Heart Health ?
NIH's National Center for Complementary and Integrative Health has this to say

Cardiovascular Diseases (CVDs) are a class of diseases that affect he heart or the blood vessels. In the United States, they are the leading cause of death. Coronary Artery disease, in which blood vessels that supply bloods to the heart become narrowed or blocked, is the most common type of CVD observed.
Several risk factors, such as age, hypertension (high blood pressure), cholesterol, have been associated to be directly linked with CVDs . Some of these may be controlled with lifestyles changes such as diet and regular exercise.
Dietary supplements, like garlic or soy, have been observed to have beneficial effects on cholesterol. Lifestyles changes such as incorporating meditation, yoga have also been observed to benefit blood pressure.
